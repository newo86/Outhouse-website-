About me

My name is Owen Lynch and I am 32 and studying web development at Griffith College. I have chosen the website for the nonprofit organization Outhouse Dublin. I am an active member of the LGBT+ community and I am passionate about supporting LGBT+ causes as well as in general supporting human rights groups. The original outhouse website is very outdated and needed to be modernized. I think the site could be utilized more by Outhouse Dublin to increase awareness ,footfall as well as to raise money for a worthy cause.
